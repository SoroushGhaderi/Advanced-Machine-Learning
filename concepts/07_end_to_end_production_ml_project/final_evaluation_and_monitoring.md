# Final Evaluation and Monitoring

**Notebook:** `07_end_to_end_production_ml_project.ipynb`

Freeze features, preprocessing, model, threshold, and business policy before opening the sealed test set. Evaluate once, report statistical and policy metrics, then deploy the versioned artifact with a model card.

After deployment, monitor schema violations, missingness, feature drift, calibration, assignment and exposure, alert volume, outcome performance, and policy value. Reliability means knowing when predictions can no longer be trusted.
